First Workshop Activity Report

The First International Workshop on the Pre-Heaven Power Method has been successfully held on October 23rd and 24th, 2010 in Skokie, Illinois, U.S.A.  Click 1st International Workshop on the Pre-Heaven Power Method for the workshop program.  Click pictures of the Pre-Heaven Power Method workshop to see some of the pictures taken during the workshop. 

Five of the participants received the certificate of Trainee Assistant.  They are Sajid Ahmed (Hanover, Illinois), Max Haid (Cleveland, Wisconsin), Everett Ho-Tom (Scarborough, Canada), Kent Kollmeyer (Chicago, Illinois), and Agustin Lopez Huete (Valencia, Spain).  The testimonials of some of the participants have been posted in the section of "Testimonials of Practitioners" of this website.
